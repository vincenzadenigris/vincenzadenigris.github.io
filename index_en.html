Love
stories

Each wedding is a love story, and my duty as a photographer is to tell step by step everything will happen during the most important day of your life.
I create your future memories about that day, I record what happens around you and your emotions with discretion.

Roberto & Dalia

Follow us into this journey which celebrates the Roberto's and Dalia's wedding.

At bride's and groom's house

I will follow you during the whole preparation, I will record each gesture, detail and emotion. I will start from the bride’s house, where the story will be divided into different phases: the makeup, the wearing, and all the exciting moments with family and friends that goes before exit from the house.

After this, I will follow the preparation of the groom and then I will go to the ceremony's place, to record the waiting for the bride. I usually adopt a reportage style, because in this way it results a more real and natural description of what happens during your special day.

The wedding

During the ceremony my job is to record everything happens step by step and with discretion. The storytelling of this moment will be made by both traditional pictures, images describing standard moments as the exchanges of the rings

and the witnesses’ signs, and other ones which will describe all your emotions in a more detailed way, but they will also show all the gestures and the looks of whom is around you during the celebration of your wedding.




Photoshooting

After the ceremony as used, I will make you a photo shooting in your preferred location. It is a brief session of shootings which aimed to take the joyful and the

happiness of your love. Also your friends can take part of it, to enhance the description of this special day: the beginning of a long and exciting story.




About me

I'm a photographer based in Andria, Bari, but ready to travel without notice. I'm focused both on commercial and fine art photography and I worked with different photographers especially for fashion and still life. I'm doing research on Narrative and staged photography, I also held workshops and lectures on it in some Italian Universities like NABA and University of San Marino, where I collaborated as assistant and I taught post production and history of contemporary art
